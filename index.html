Handcrafted Timber Work Built for Irish Homes

Coy Timber & Design is a family-run Irish woodworking business based in County Wexford. We specialise in high-quality custom pergolas, outdoor timber structures, and bespoke woodwork designed to suit your home, garden, and lifestyle.

Every project is built with care, using durable materials and traditional craftsmanship, ensuring your timber work not only looks great but lasts for years in the Irish climate.
